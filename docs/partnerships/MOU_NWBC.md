## Preamble

The Northwest Baptist Convention (hereinafter "NWBC") and HebronGuide LLC (hereinafter "HebronGuide") enter into this Memorandum of Understanding based on a shared commitment to serving Korean-American immigrants throughout the Pacific Northwest.

NWBC serves churches across Washington, Oregon, Idaho, and Alaska, equipping local congregations to fulfill the Great Commission and care for their communities.

HebronGuide is a digital settlement platform that helps Korean-American immigrants find practical information, community, and faith connections in their new cities.

Both organizations are united by Matthew 25:35: *"I was a stranger and you welcomed me."*

---

## Article 1 — Purpose

1. To connect NWBC-affiliated Korean and Korean-friendly churches with newly arrived Korean immigrants via HebronGuide.
2. To equip NWBC churches to serve Korean immigrant communities more effectively.
3. To create a sustainable referral network that benefits both Korean immigrants and Pacific Northwest churches.

---

## Article 2 — NWBC Commitments

### 2-1. Ministry Commitments
- Provide a list of NWBC-affiliated Korean and Korean-friendly churches (updated semi-annually)
- Distribute HebronGuide QR codes at NWBC annual meetings, pastors' conferences, and regional events
- Encourage member churches to display HebronGuide materials for newcomers
- Recommend HebronGuide to churches seeking to serve Korean immigrant populations

### 2-2. Financial Support
NWBC agrees to provide the following financial partnership contribution:

| Item | Amount | Schedule |
|---|---|---|
| Annual Partnership Fee | $1,500 | January each year |
| Optional: Event Co-sponsorship | $500/event | Per event |

> Note: All NWBC-affiliated Korean churches are exempt from individual registration ($50) and annual ($20) fees.

---

## Article 3 — HebronGuide Commitments

### 3-1. Platform Services
- Automatic Tier 2 listing for all NWBC-affiliated Korean and Korean-friendly churches
- "NWBC Partner Church" official badge displayed on all listings
- Priority placement within Pacific Northwest city pages (Seattle, Portland, etc.)
- New immigrant matching: connect Korean newcomers with nearby NWBC churches

### 3-2. Data & Reporting
- Monthly report: church page views and new immigrant connection metrics
- Annual partnership impact report

### 3-3. Content Partnership
- NWBC events and announcements published on HebronGuide (complimentary)
- HebronGuide settlement guides reference NWBC church resources

---

## Article 4 — Listing Principles

Financial contribution size does not determine listing placement. Placement is determined solely by HebronGuide's theological verification Tier system, applied consistently and transparently.

---

## Article 5 — Term & Renewal

- Term: One (1) year from signing
- Auto-renewal: Unless either party provides written notice 30 days before expiration
- Amendment: Requires written agreement from both parties

---

## Article 6 — Termination

Either party may terminate this MOU with 30 days written notice, or immediately in case of:
- Material breach of this agreement
- Theological or organizational direction that fundamentally conflicts with this agreement's purpose

Upon termination, any prepaid fees will be refunded on a pro-rata basis.

---

## Article 7 — Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of shared church data, contact information, and financial terms.
