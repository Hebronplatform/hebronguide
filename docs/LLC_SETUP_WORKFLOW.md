### STEP 1B: Washington LLC 신청 ⏱ 15분

**🔗 신청 사이트**: https://ccfs.sos.wa.gov/

**입력할 정보 (목사님이 직접 입력):**

```
─────────────────────────────────────────
법인명: Hebron Platform LLC
유형: Limited Liability Company (LLC)
관할: Washington State

주소 (Principal Office):
  [교회 주소 또는 자택 주소 사용 가능]
  반드시 워싱턴주 주소

Registered Agent (등록 대리인):
  ✅ 본인 (Paul Kim) - 추천
  주소: 위와 동일
  
관리 구조: Member-Managed (회원 직접 운영)
회원: Paul Kim (본인 1인)

목적 (Purpose):
  "Operating an online platform providing settlement, 
  community, and resource information for Korean immigrants 
  and Korean Americans across North American cities."

설립 유효일: 즉시 (filing date)

비용: $200 (온라인 신청)
처리 기간: 2-3 영업일
─────────────────────────────────────────
```

**필요한 것:**
- 신용카드 ($200 결제용)
- 이메일 (확인서 받을 곳)
- 워싱턴주 주소

**완료 후 받는 것:**
- UBI 번호 (Unified Business Identifier)
- Articles of Organization PDF
- Certificate of Formation

### STEP 1C: EIN 신청 (LLC 승인 후) ⏱ 10분

**시점**: LLC 승인 확인서 받은 직후 (보통 2-3일)

**🔗 신청 사이트**: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online

⚠️ **반드시 이 공식 IRS 사이트에서만!** (사기 사이트 많음 — $200 청구하는 곳들 다 가짜)

**입력 정보:**
```
─────────────────────────────────────────
Legal Name: Hebron Platform LLC
Trade Name (DBA): HebronGuide (선택사항)
Mailing Address: [LLC 등록 주소와 동일]
Responsible Party: Paul Kim (본인 SSN 입력)
Type of Entity: Limited Liability Company (LLC)
Number of Members: 1
State LLC Formed In: Washington
Reason for Applying: Started new business
Principal Activity: Other Information Services
Principal Products: Online community guide platform
─────────────────────────────────────────
```

**비용**: 무료 (절대 돈 받는 곳은 사기)  
**처리 기간**: 즉시 발급 (PDF 다운로드 가능)  
**완료 후**: EIN 9자리 번호 받음 (예: 12-3456789)

## 📅 이번 주 (3-5일차) — 은행 계좌 개설

### STEP 2: Chase Business Checking 개설 ⏱ 1시간 (지점 방문)

**시점**: LLC 승인서 + EIN 둘 다 받은 후

**준비물:**
1. ✅ Articles of Organization (PDF 인쇄)
2. ✅ EIN 확인서 (CP 575 letter, PDF)
3. ✅ Operating Agreement (아래 템플릿 사용)
4. ✅ 본인 운전면허증
5. ✅ 본인 SSN
6. ✅ LLC 주소 증명 (영업장 주소)
7. ✅ 초기 입금 ($25 이상)

**Chase 추천 이유:**
- 교회가 Chase 계좌면 즉시 이체 편리
- $300 신규 가입 보너스 자주 있음 (조건 확인)
- 전국 ATM 다수
- 모바일 앱 우수

**대안 은행:**
- WA Federal Credit Union (한인 친화적)
- BECU (워싱턴주 신협, 수수료 낮음)
- UniBank 유니뱅크 (한국계 은행, 한국어 지원)

## 📋 Operating Agreement 템플릿 (1인 LLC)

> 별도 공증 불필요. 본인 서명만으로 효력 발생.
> 출력해서 서명 후 LLC 서류와 함께 보관.

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
       OPERATING AGREEMENT OF HEBRONGUIDE LLC
       A Single-Member Washington Limited Liability Company
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DATED: ____________, 2026

ARTICLE 1 — FORMATION

1.1 Formation. Hebron Platform LLC (the "Company") was formed as a 
Washington Limited Liability Company under the Washington Limited 
Liability Company Act on the date the Articles of Organization 
were filed with the Washington Secretary of State.

1.2 Name. The Company's legal name is "Hebron Platform LLC."

1.3 Principal Office. The principal office of the Company is 
located at: [주소], Washington, [우편번호].

1.4 Registered Agent. The Company's registered agent in 
Washington is Paul Kim, with the address [주소].

1.5 Purpose. The Company is organized for the purpose of operating 
an online platform providing settlement, community, and resource 
information for Korean immigrants and Korean Americans across 
North American cities, and any other lawful business.

1.6 Term. The Company shall continue in existence until 
dissolved in accordance with this Agreement.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ARTICLE 2 — MEMBERSHIP

2.1 Sole Member. The sole member of the Company is Paul Kim 
("Member"), holding 100% of the membership interest.

2.2 Capital Contribution. The initial capital contribution of 
the Member is $_______________ (initial deposit amount).

2.3 No Additional Members. No additional members shall be 
admitted to the Company without an amendment to this Agreement.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ARTICLE 3 — MANAGEMENT

3.1 Member-Managed. The Company is managed by its sole Member, 
Paul Kim.

3.2 Authority. The Member has full authority to manage and 
control the business and affairs of the Company, including but 
not limited to:
  (a) Opening and managing bank accounts
  (b) Entering into contracts
  (c) Receiving payments and making distributions
  (d) Hiring contractors and service providers
  (e) Filing taxes and making elections

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ARTICLE 4 — DISTRIBUTIONS AND TAXATION

4.1 Distributions. All profits and losses of the Company shall 
be allocated to the sole Member.

4.2 Tax Treatment. The Company shall be treated as a 
disregarded entity for federal income tax purposes (default for 
single-member LLC). The Member shall report all Company income 
and expenses on Schedule C of their personal federal tax return.

4.3 Distributions Discretion. The Member may, at any time, 
distribute available cash to themselves.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ARTICLE 5 — SEPARATION FROM AFFILIATED ENTITIES

5.1 Independent Operation. Hebron Platform LLC operates as an 
independent business entity, separate and distinct from any 
religious organization or church, including Global Mission 
Church of Greater Seattle.

5.2 No Comingling. Funds, assets, and operations of the Company 
shall be maintained strictly separate from any affiliated 
religious organization. The Company shall maintain its own 
bank accounts, accounting records, and contracts.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ARTICLE 6 — DISSOLUTION

6.1 Events of Dissolution. The Company shall be dissolved upon 
the occurrence of any of the following:
  (a) Written decision of the Member
  (b) Conversion to a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization
  (c) Any event causing dissolution under Washington law

6.2 Winding Up. Upon dissolution, the Member shall wind up the 
affairs of the Company, pay outstanding debts, and distribute 
remaining assets to the Member.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ARTICLE 7 — MISCELLANEOUS

7.1 Amendments. This Agreement may be amended only by written 
consent of the Member.

7.2 Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws 
of the State of Washington.

7.3 Severability. If any provision of this Agreement is found 
unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in 
effect.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned Member has executed this 
Operating Agreement as of the date first written above.


__________________________________
Paul Kim, Sole Member
Hebron Platform LLC

Date: _________________________

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

## 📊 진행 체크리스트

```
□ Step 1A: 법인명 결정 (Hebron Platform LLC)
□ Step 1B: WA LLC 신청 ($200) — corps.wa.gov
□ Step 1B: LLC 승인서 받기 (2-3일 후)
□ Step 1C: EIN 신청 (무료) — irs.gov
□ Step 1C: EIN 확인서 받기 (즉시)
□ Step 2: Operating Agreement 출력·서명
□ Step 2: Chase Business 계좌 개설 (지점 방문)
□ Step 3: 인보이스 #001, #002 작성·전송
□ Step 4: 기존 결제 $200 → LLC 계좌로 이체
□ Step 5: 향후 모든 수익 → LLC 계좌만 사용
```

## ⚠️ 주의사항

1. **개인 SSN 보호**: EIN 신청에는 본인 SSN 필요. 절대 비공식 사이트 사용 금지
2. **이메일 보관**: LLC·EIN 확인서 모두 별도 폴더에 영구 보관
3. **회계 분리**: 첫날부터 모든 수익·지출을 LLC 계좌로만 처리
4. **연간 보고**: 매년 4월경 WA Annual Report ($70) 잊지 말기 — 알림 등록
